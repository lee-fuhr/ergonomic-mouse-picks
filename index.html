How we choose our picks

We don’t unbox a new mouse every week or run stopwatch tests. Instead, we dig into years of user feedback on forums, retail reviews, and published long-term tests from trusted tech-review sites. We look for patterns: which models still track smoothly after six months, which rubber coatings peel, which batteries actually last as claimed. Our picks are the ones with a clear consensus across thousands of owners, not just launch-week buzz. We prioritize comfort across common hand sizes, real-world durability, and the availability of replacement parts. If a mouse has a known scroll-wheel failure after a year, it doesn’t make the list, no matter how good it feels on day one.
